About

Fireflies is a tiny web-app that displays a hand-picked quote and background image each time you refresh the page.

This project was inspired by Chibird's posts on Tumblr. One of her posts in particular stood out to me, and soon after Fireflies was born.

I created Fireflies so I could learn JavaScript, and along the way I discovered how useful and amazing things like AngularJS, NPM, Bower, and Grunt can be.
