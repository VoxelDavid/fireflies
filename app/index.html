About Fireflies

Fireflies is a little single-page application built off of AngularJS, with NPM and Bower handling my dependencies, and Grunt running tasks to automate my build process – amongst other things.

I was inspired to make Fireflies by about:mozilla and the Tumblr user Chibird. I loved some of her posts and I wanted to incorporate them into something, and with about:mozilla giving me a nice idea of where to start, Fireflies was born.
